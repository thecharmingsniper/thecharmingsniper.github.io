Origin

The name came first.

"My friends called me Sniper long before I turned it into anything. They gave me the name because I catch everything — the tiny detail that changed since last time, the face across a packed room, the flicker when someone's words don't match what they're feeling."

What We Stand For

Seven values.
No compromises.

01 — Love
Love

02 — Acceptance
Acceptance
Take yourself exactly how you're built, then sharpen it. Nobody here has to mask to belong.

03 — Authenticity
Authenticity
Real and rough beats polished and fake every time. If you can't say it straight, don't say it at all.
✦

Justice

Awareness

06 — Discipline
Discipline
Wild energy that isn't aimed just burns you down. Train the body, sharpen the skills, guard the mind. Daily choice.
